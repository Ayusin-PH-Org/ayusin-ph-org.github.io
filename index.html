Why It Matters

Ayusin.ph gives every citizen a direct line to local government and makes every step of the response visible.

For LGUs, it's a unified intake and case management tool that reduces noise, surfaces urgency, and builds public trust through transparency.

Who Benefits

  • Citizens: simple reporting and real-time status
  • LGUs: centralized intake, assignments, SLAs, analytics

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can see my report?

Public reports are visible by default. Sensitive topics can be submitted privately to the LGU when appropriate.

How are duplicates handled?

Citizens are prompted to upvote similar nearby reports. LGUs can merge duplicates to keep workloads clean.

What about data privacy?

We align with the Philippine Data Privacy Act, apply encryption, and anonymize public datasets.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Reports draft offline and sync when a connection is available (mobile apps).
